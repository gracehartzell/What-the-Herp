Snake Specific

  • What to do if a snake attacks you?

    • It didn't. You provoked it and it defended itself. Also take a peek at the snake if you can so that you can help medical professionals assess the potential risk from the bite.

  • A venomous snake bit me, am I going to die?

    • Probably not, but you should still take all preventative measures to mitigate potential damages. The majority of venomous snakes are broken into elapids and vipers.
